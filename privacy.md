# Privacy Policy for Crash Out

**Effective Date:** March 2026

**Overview:**
The Crash Out app is designed as a local-only utility for personal motivation and stress relief.

**Data Collection:**
* **No Personal Data:** We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
* **No Input Storage:** Any text entered into the "vent" box is processed locally on your device and is permanently deleted the moment the "Release" animation occurs. We do not store, transmit, or share your entries.

**Contact:**
For any questions, please contact the developer via GitHub.
